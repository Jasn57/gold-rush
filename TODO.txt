# Core Gameplay

Mine coal and ores ✅

Smelt ores using coal as fuel ✅

Sell bars for money ✅

Use money to buy better pickaxes ✅

Durability for picks

Hire miners to automate mining

Prestige to reset for permanent bonuses

# Economy & Progression

Market with fluctuating gold bar prices

Random trader who offers better deals occasionally

Smelting efficiency upgrades (use less coal or faster smelt time)

Mining power upgrades (mine more ore per click)

Storage limits with purchasable expansions

Bank interest or investments to earn passive income

Crafting system (make jewelry or refined products for higher profits)

# Resources & Items

Add new ores: Iron, Silver, Platinum, etc.

Add rare gems that boost multipliers or unlock special upgrades

Fuel alternatives later in game (like oil or magic fuel)

# Upgrades & Automation

Miner automation tiers (apprentice → foreman → industrial drill)

Smelter automation (auto-process ores using stored fuel)

Repair system for worn-out tools (uses money or materials)

Power plant upgrade to reduce smelting costs

1 upgrade button for picks rather than multiple buttons 

# Extras & Replayability

Achievements and milestones

Random events (e.g., “Gold Rush” for double income for 30 seconds)

Mining levels or XP system

Leaderboard (local high scores)

Challenges or timed goals

Offline progress (earn resources while away)

# UI & Quality of Life

Local storage ✅

Manual save/load and reset options

Progress bars for mining/smelting

Tooltip info for upgrades and prices

Dark mode and color themes

Sound effects and background music
